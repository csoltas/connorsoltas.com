This is me (with a face I make often) ↓

As a designer with a math background, I'm fascinated by interfaces for technical products and technical problem spaces. Especially ones with real-world stakes, or competing user interests that need to be balanced.

About half my work is designing the mechanics of those systems -- like pricing structures and matching algorithms -- and half is actual user interface.

From 2017 to 2023, I designed products and led cross-team initiatives for Uber. Before that, I was a Kleiner Perkins Design Fellow (2016). Before that, I worked at VC firms and startup accelerators, wearing hats as an engineer, designer, researcher, and PM. Before that, I studied applied math, computer science, and economics at the University of Chicago. Here's my resume↗.

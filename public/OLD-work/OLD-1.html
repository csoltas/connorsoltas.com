On setting a vision (and actually getting some of it built)

Uber 2021-2022 Design lead & product strategy

In 2021, I was one of two designers asked to lead a redesign of Uber's complex and cumbersome driver app. Despite internal support for our vision, at its core was a map overhaul that Uber was in no position to execute, either organizationally or architecturally. I proposed and led an org-wide initiative to change that.

A cross-functional initiative to bring it to life

To overhaul the map as we envisioned, many teams would need to collaborate on a tech migration and a new design. But the map had no clear product owner to lead that collaboration -- surprisingly, since it's 75% of the app home screen. So I became its owner. I brokered conversations between four teams with competing short-term incentives, presented to execs, and led those teams with a unified plan.

Vision-setting and vision-enabling

In 2021, I was one of two designers asked to lead a redesign of Uber's complex and cumbersome driver app.

Despite internal support for our vision, at its core was a map overhaul that Uber was in no position to execute, either organizationally or architecturally.

I proposed and led an initiative across teams to change that.

A cross-functional initiative to bring it to life

At our vision's core was a stark simplification of the app's main map -- a surface with no clear owner, despite being 75% of the app home screen.

So I became its owner. I brokered conversations between teams with competing short-term incentives, presented to execs, and led those teams with a unified plan.

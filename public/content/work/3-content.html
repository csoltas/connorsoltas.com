Rapid results on an aggressive timeline

Uber drivers use the driver app's Activity Feed every day to review their recent trips and earnings.

But its design buried that use case deep, forcing users to drill down into each individual receipt for the info they need.

In 2020 I led a one-month rethink of the feature, which had a dramatic effect both on engagement (+41%) and on that painful drill-down behavior (−38%).

Take a peek at the new design in action ↓

To meet our aggressive timeline, I worked directly with the VP of Driver and design leadership, instead of a conventional review process.

Meanwhile I worked with Engineering to ensure my UI framework would extend to the team's future plans. I also updated the Activity Feed's visual language to Uber's then-new design system.

The rapid progress motivated a central focus on Earnings UI in the team's 2021 roadmap.

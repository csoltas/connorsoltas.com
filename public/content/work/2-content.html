Building design tools to break out of old thinking

Uber 2022-2023 Design & engineering lead

In late 2022, I conceived and built a tool for Uber designers to work on cartography and geospatial visualizations in tandem. That tool helped me lead a redesign of the Uber driver/courier app's most used feature -- the main map -- from the ground up. The tooling I built sped up the redesign while improving the quality of every stage of output.

This infrastructure allowed me to explore more radical ideas faster, at high fidelity, with production code as a deliverable. The new design -- which was a collaboration with one other designer, who led the non-map UI -- completely reshaped the map into a stark canvas for data visualization. This launch is still under development at Uber, but at the time I left, it had been presented company-wide and even in board meetings.

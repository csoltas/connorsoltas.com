Hey, that's me ↓

I'm fascinated by the interface design problems faced by emerging technologies -- problems where the user behaviors and the norms that typically constrain a solution space aren't well-defined yet. Where “what you choose to design” is almost as important as “how you design it.”

And as a product designer with a math background, I try to go beyond the bounds of conventional design efforts to solve those problems. About half my work is interface design -- information architecture, design systems, technical prototyping, usability testing -- and half is other stuff like building my own tooling, combing through experiment data, leading cross-functional teams, and so on.

From 2017 to 2023, I designed products and led cross-team initiatives for Uber. Before that, I was a Kleiner Perkins Design Fellow (2016). Before that, I worked at VC firms and startup accelerators, wearing hats as an engineer, designer, researcher, and PM.
